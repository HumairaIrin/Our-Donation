Our Donation Plan

We will try our level best to reach the families who needs us the most, As soon as we could.

Food for families

The body needs the nutrients in food to survive.Without food, the body starts to use its own tissue as fuel, but it can't do this for so long.As they are in trouble and don't have enough food.We decided to give food to them.

Medicines for families

The people who are suffering they might not be in a situation to buy medicines for them.We will give some basic medicines. So that they can get over from the common health issues.

Shelter for families

Most of the families lost their house during this flood.Understanding their need we will make them new house or take other step for them.
